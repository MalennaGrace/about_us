About Me:

My name is Aaron Jef N. Barcega, an IT student focused on building my own empire. Balancing work and studies has been a constant journey for me, as I strive to turn my dreams into reality. One of my greatest passions is motorcycles, and each one I own has a special story. My black Honda Wave is my reliable companion for delivering parcels; it’s incredibly efficient and helps me with my job. Then there’s my red motorcycle—a classic bike that I gifted myself as a reward for my hard work and to fulfill a long-time dream. Finally, there’s my Kawasaki Ninja, my ultimate dream bike. Achieving it was a milestone, but it’s only the beginning—I won’t stop until I achieve my next goal: owning my dream car.
