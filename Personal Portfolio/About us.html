About Us:

This website is created as part of our final project submission. We, Malenna, Rona, Ashley, Nadine, Aaron and Zedrick are a team of passionate individuals who came together to share a little more about ourselves with you. We all met at the beginning of our first year of college, initially as a group tasked with completing a project assigned by our professor. As time went on, we grew closer and eventually we became friends. Over time, we developed a bond built on humor, shared interests and complain about the things we hate, and offer support to each other when it comes to our studies. Through this website, we hope to offer a glimpse into our personal information about ourselves and the things we are interested in. We invite you to explore and hope you find our website both engaging and enjoyable.
